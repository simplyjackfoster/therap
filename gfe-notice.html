Your right to a Good Faith Estimate.

This notice describes your rights to receive a Good Faith Estimate (GFE) for the cost of mental health care services if you are uninsured or choose self-pay.

About GFEs

Who receives a Good Faith Estimate?

If you are uninsured or choose to pay out of pocket (self-pay) for services, you are entitled to a Good Faith Estimate that details the expected costs of your care. This applies to counseling and coaching services offered by Teresa R. Foster, L.C.S.W.

What’s included

Information you will receive

  • Itemized list of anticipated services and fees
  • Diagnosis codes (if applicable) and CPT codes
  • Estimated total cost of services for a 12-month period
  • Instructions for requesting updates or additional estimates
