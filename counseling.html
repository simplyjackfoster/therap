Counseling in Bloomington & via telehealth

Relational, trauma-informed therapy for your next chapter.

Counseling with Teresa R. Foster, L.C.S.W. supports nervous-system regulation, emotional processing, and values-driven action. We’ll co-create a pace that feels safe and sustainable for diverse lived experiences.

Book a 20-min consultation

Therapy services are for adults located in Indiana. In emergencies, call 911 or 988.

Therapeutic focus areas

Support for anxiety, trauma, and life transitions

Sessions are 55 minutes and grounded in attachment-based and somatic therapies. We will slow down, notice patterns, and practice nervous-system tools that create room for choice. Treatment plans are reviewed regularly and aligned with your informed consent.

  • Trauma healing & EMDR
  • Anxiety & perfectionism
  • Grief & loss
  • Identity exploration & diversity-affirming care
  • Burnout & boundaries
  • Couples navigating transitions

Modalities we may integrate

  • Eye Movement Desensitization & Reprocessing (EMDR) for reprocessing memories and regulating the nervous system
  • Somatic and polyvagal-informed practices
  • Mindfulness, compassion, and values work
What to expect

Your first sessions

1. Consultation

We start with a complimentary 20-minute phone call to review your goals, answer questions, and assess goodness of fit.

2. Intake & planning

Complete intake forms through the secure client portal. The first 1–2 sessions explore history, strengths, and co-create a treatment plan.

3. Ongoing therapy

Sessions are collaborative, paced with your nervous system, and grounded in empathy and accountability to support real growth.

Please avoid sending protected health information by email. Clinical paperwork, scheduling, and messaging take place within the HIPAA-compliant portal. Review the fees & insurance guide for billing details.

Ethics & scope

Clear boundaries support effective care

I practice within the NASW Code of Ethics and maintain active consultation to ensure culturally responsive and trauma-informed care. Therapy is available to adults who are physically located in Indiana at the time of service. If you reside outside Indiana, please explore the coaching offerings, which are not mental health treatment.

We will coordinate with your care team—with your consent—when collaboration enhances outcomes (e.g., psychiatrists or medical providers). If we determine a different level of care is needed, I will provide referrals.

Crisis & urgent support

Counseling sessions are not designed for emergency response. If you need immediate help, contact:

  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline
  • Monroe County Crisis Line: (812) 331-2011
  • Call 911 or visit the nearest emergency room
