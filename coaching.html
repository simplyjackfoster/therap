Coaching pillars

For people ready to step into the next version of themselves

Coaching is individualized for people stepping into what’s next professionally or personally. Through reflective exercises and real-world action, we create a path that aligns with your capacity, values, and the life you’re building.

  • Leadership transitions
  • Burnout recovery plans
  • Values & boundary work
  • Purpose & Career Direction
  • Strategic communication

How coaching differs from therapy

Coaching focuses on present and future goals, while therapy addresses mental health diagnoses, trauma processing, and clinical concerns.

  • Goal-oriented accountability and experiments
  • Available to clients located in states where coaching is permitted
  • Referrals provided if therapeutic support becomes necessary
Coaching packages

Select a format that supports your goals

1) Foundations Package

The Alignment Starter

Four 60-minute sessions over eight weeks. A space to get grounded, understand patterns, and build capacity for intentional change. This is ideal for someone who knows something needs to shift, but isn’t sure where to begin.

$875 — includes session notes + personalized practices

2) Reset Package

The Transition Reset

A focused container for people in a life or career crossroads. We clarify values, explore options, and map next-step experiments rooted in purpose rather than pressure.

$1,050 — Five 60-minute sessions over ten weeks, includes between-session accountability check-ins

3) Momentum Package

Small Steps → Big Shifts

A gentle but structured coaching arc for people who want steady progress without feeling overwhelmed. We break goals into doable experiments and review what shifts.

$1,200 — Six 60-minute sessions over three months

4) Direction Package

Pathfinding + Decision Clarity

Ideal for someone choosing between opportunities or exploring new identity chapters — career, location, relationships, purpose. Structured decision frameworks + reflective coaching.

$750 — Three 75-minute sessions across six weeks, includes optional journaling prompts + integration week

5) Deepening Package

Sustained Growth Support

For clients who complete a sprint and want continued guidance without a retainer. Keeps momentum, integrates new habits, and deepens confidence in direction.

$450/month — Monthly: 2 sessions + email support, cancel anytime with 30 days’ notice

Coaching fees are not eligible for insurance reimbursement. Payment plans are available. Sliding-scale therapy is reserved for counseling clients and follows a separate process.

Process

How coaching engagements unfold

Discovery

We begin with a 20-minute consultation to clarify needs, confirm scope, and choose the package that fits.

Design

The first session maps values, vision, and obstacles. We outline experiments and metrics for momentum.

Integration

Each meeting closes with commitments and check-ins. We track energy, celebrate growth, and adjust as needed.
