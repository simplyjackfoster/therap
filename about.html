About Teresa

I am a licensed clinical social worker who truly enjoys listening and assisting people with their goals of transition in life. I find it important to examine not just past history, but to look at strengths and current situations of the individual. I have worked in the school setting, treatment facilities and in homes of families for over twenty years.
