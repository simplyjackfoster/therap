Therapeutic approach

Grounded, integrative, and ethics-aligned care

I am a Licensed Clinical Social Worker and EMDR therapist with more than 20 years of experience supporting adults, students, and professionals. My practice integrates attachment theory, somatic awareness, and practical skill-building. Sessions are collaborative and attuned to the intersections of identity, systems, and trauma.

I honor the NASW Code of Ethics, provide transparent informed consent, and never guarantee outcomes. Together we design a plan that centers your autonomy and values.

  • EMDR
  • Culturally responsive, diversity-affirming care
  • Polyvagal-informed care
  • Self-compassion practice
  • Executive coaching frameworks
