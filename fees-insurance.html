  • Are telehealth sessions covered when I am located in Indiana?
Good Faith Estimate

Your rights under the No Surprises Act

You have the right to receive a Good Faith Estimate (GFE) explaining how much your medical and mental health care is expected to cost. This applies to clients who are uninsured or who choose self-pay. You will receive a written GFE at least one business day before your scheduled service and can request an updated estimate at any time.

If you receive a bill that is at least $400 more than your GFE, you may dispute the bill. For questions, visit CMS No Surprises Act resources or call (800) 985-3059.
