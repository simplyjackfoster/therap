Accessibility

We are committed to inclusive, accessible care.

This website and practice follow WCAG 2.2 AA guidelines wherever possible. Please reach out if you encounter barriers so we can improve your experience.

Web accessibility

Standards & testing

This site was designed with semantic HTML, keyboard navigation, and accessible color contrast aligned with WCAG 2.2 AA guidelines. Automated testing with axe DevTools and manual keyboard checks were conducted on desktop and mobile breakpoints. We support screen readers by providing descriptive alt text, skip links, focus indicators, and logical heading order.

Reasonable accommodations

Need assistance?

If you need any materials in alternate formats (large print, audio, translated documents) or require accommodations for in-person sessions, email tfosterlcsw@gmail.com. We will respond within two business days.

Physical accessibility

Office information

The downtown Bloomington office includes elevator access, accessible restrooms, and nearby parking. If you require additional accommodations, please let us know before your appointment so we can prepare the space or recommend telehealth alternatives.

Feedback

How to report an issue

We welcome feedback about accessibility barriers or improvement ideas. Contact us at tfosterlcsw@gmail.com. We aim to respond within two business days and resolve issues promptly.
