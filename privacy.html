Privacy

Your privacy matters.

This statement outlines how Teresa R. Foster, L.C.S.W. collects, uses, and protects information shared through this website and in clinical services.

Email privacy questions Access the secure portal
Website

Information collected online

This website collects minimal personal information. Analytics events capture anonymous usage data (e.g., page views, button clicks) to improve the experience. IP addresses are anonymized via Google Analytics 4 configuration. Cookies are not used for advertising or tracking across sites.

The contact form sends messages via email; please avoid sharing protected health information (PHI). Clinical forms and messaging occur in the secure client portal.

Clinical records

How health information is protected

Client records are maintained in a HIPAA-compliant electronic health record with role-based access, encryption in transit and at rest, and audit logging. Records are retained for at least seven years as required by Indiana law. You may request copies or amendments to your records in writing.

Confidentiality limits

When information may be shared

Information may be disclosed with your written consent or when required by law, including suspected abuse or neglect, court orders, or imminent risk of harm to self or others. For insurance billing, necessary information may be shared with payers to obtain reimbursement.

Your rights

Access & questions

You may request access to your records, ask questions about privacy practices, or file a complaint without retaliation. Contact tfosterlcsw@gmail.com or call (812) 320-3326. Complaints can also be submitted to the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services Office for Civil Rights.
